About Eco Banano

Eco Banano is a student-led business dedicated to sustainability. We collect banana pseudo-stems and convert them into reusable and multipurpose materials. Instead of letting agricultural waste go to landfill or be burned, we turn it into something useful, championing the circular economy.

Our Mission

Eco Banano aims to secure a significant share of the banana-based product market by prioritizing **sustainable and ethical sourcing**. This is followed by consistent production and distribution, establishing reliable supply chains and community partnerships.

Our Vision

The long-term vision focuses on **sustained growth, market expansion**, and building a strong brand reputation for high-quality, sustainable, and innovative banana-based products that inspire others to reduce waste and choose eco-friendly alternatives.

Our Eco-Friendly Process

See the steps we take to transform agricultural waste into valuable, multipurpose materials.

1. Collection & Sourcing

We gather discarded banana pseudo-stems from local farmers, ensuring we use what would otherwise be waste.

2. Fiber Extraction

The coarse, natural fibers are carefully separated from the stem using a manual or semi-mechanical process.

3. Natural Drying

The extracted fibers are naturally air and sun-dried to remove all moisture and maintain their natural strength.

4. Pulping & Processing

Fibers are pulped, cleaned, and treated using eco-friendly methods to prepare them for material formation.

5. Forming & Shaping

The prepared pulp is pressed and formed into sheets, ropes, or bags, depending on the final product.

6. Quality Finishing

The final products are inspected, trimmed, and packaged, ensuring high quality and durability for the customer.
